Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2024

1. Data Collection

Bones & Balance Physical Therapy ("we", "us", or "our") collects personal information including but not limited to name, contact details, medical history, and insurance information specifically for the purpose of providing physical therapy services.

2. HIPAA Compliance

We are fully committed to protecting your health information. All medical data is handled in strict accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We use secure, encrypted servers for all patient portal communications and treatment records.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We only share your information with third parties (such as insurance providers or specialist physicians) when necessary for your treatment or insurance reimbursement, and only with your explicit consent.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your medical records, request corrections to your information, and revoke consent for data sharing at any time.
